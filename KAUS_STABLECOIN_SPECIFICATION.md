## ğŸ”„ Minting & Burning

### Minting Process
1. User deposits USD to FIELD NINE
2. USD held in reserve account
3. Equivalent KAUS minted (1:1 ratio)
4. KAUS sent to user's wallet
5. Reserve updated in real-time

### Burning Process
1. User requests redemption
2. KAUS burned from supply
3. USD released from reserves
4. USD sent to user's bank account
5. Reserve updated in real-time

### Fees
- **Minting Fee**: 0.1% (minimum $10)
- **Burning Fee**: 0.1% (minimum $10)
- **Transaction Fee**: 0.001 KAUS per transaction
